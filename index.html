Some common questions
were often asked

How do I prevent my makeup from looking cakey?

To avoid cakey makeup, start with a well-moisturized face, apply a lightweight primer, and use thin layers of foundation. Blend well and set with a light powder or setting spray to maintain a natural finish.

Why does my lipstick fade so quickly?

Lipstick fades faster if lips are dry or if too much product is applied. Exfoliate your lips, apply a lip primer or liner, and layer the lipstick with a blotting technique for longer wear.

What causes my mascara to smudge?

Mascara smudges due to oily eyelids or excessive blinking before it dries. Use a waterproof formula, apply a light layer of powder on your eyelids, and avoid touching your eyes throughout the day.

How can I make my foundation last all day?

Prep your skin with a primer, use a long-wear foundation, and set it with translucent powder and setting spray. Blot excess oil throughout the day instead of adding more product.

Why does my concealer crease under my eyes?

Concealer creases when too much product is applied or if the under-eye area is too dry. Use an eye cream, apply a thin layer of concealer, and set it lightly with powder to prevent creasing.

How do I choose the right blush for my skin tone?

Fair skin suits soft pinks and peaches, medium skin looks great with warm corals, while deep skin tones pop with rich berries and plums. Always blend well for a natural flush.
